SunGrow Coconut Shell Ladder Hut Bird House

If you wish to watch birds playing in their house the way they do naturally in the wild, hang SunGrow Coconut Shell Ladder Hut Bird House and put bird food inside. This small birdhouse is made with a coconut shell with a wooden ladder. Hence it is absolutely natural, and it is weather resistance. You can offer it to small wild birds as well as your small pet at home. It is good for small birds like sparrow, parakeets, robins, lovebirds, and finch.
