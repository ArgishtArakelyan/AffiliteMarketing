Forti-Diet Pro Health Parakeet Bird Food, 5 -lb bag.rtf.doc 2.12 kB

This product will instantly stop bleeding from small cuts in birds and other pets. Remedy + Recovery Stop bleeding styptic Powder will make blood clots readily in minor injury caused by nails clipping or cut through the skin. Another good thing is that it doesn't work only for birds but also many pets including cats and dogs. This styptic powder does not contain alcohol, hence it will not cause irritation or sharp pain.

Benefits

Stop bleeding caused by minor cuts

It is useful for many pets.

Causes blood to clot instantly.

It does not contain any alcohol.

How To Use

Apply on a moistened swab or any other applicator and press gently on the bleeding part until it stops bleeding, or put a pinch directly on the small wound.

Precautions

Only use for small cuts and never for a deep wound or burns.

Do not apply in a body cavity.

Consult a veterinary doctor if bleeding persists after use.
